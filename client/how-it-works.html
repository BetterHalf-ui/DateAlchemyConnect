The Process in Detail

01

Apply for Membership

You'll begin by completing our initial application: a questionnaire that helps us understand your background, values, and what you're truly seeking in a partner. Within one business day, we'll review your submission and share an in-depth assessment.

02

Private Consultation

Meet with your personal matchmaker in a one-on-one video consultation. This conversation helps us understand who you are and what you're looking for. We craft a profile summary for selective sharing with potential matches.

03

Curated Introduction

Once a mutually aligned connection is identified, we share both profiles for review. If there's mutual interest, we activate your membership and arrange your first date with care and discretion.

04

Refinement & Follow-up

After the introduction, the path is yours — whether it's a second date, exchanging contact details, or exploring a new introduction. We seek your feedback to fine-tune our matching approach.
